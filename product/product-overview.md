# TeqBook

## Description
TeqBook is a clean, modern management platform designed specifically for barbershops and beauty salons. It streamlines day-to-day operations by bringing booking, scheduling, employee management, and business insights together in one intuitive system.

## Problems & Solutions

### Problem 1: Manual Booking Chaos
TeqBook's digital booking system allows customers to book appointments online while staff manage schedules in real-time, eliminating double-bookings and reducing no-shows.

### Problem 2: Disorganized Scheduling
The integrated calendar and shift management system helps owners assign staff efficiently, manage availability, and optimize coverage across busy periods.

### Problem 3: Scattered Customer Information
A centralized customer database tracks service history, preferences, and records, enabling personalized experiences and better customer relationships.

### Problem 4: Financial Tracking Difficulties
Integrated billing and comprehensive reporting provide clear revenue insights, service performance metrics, and financial analytics to help owners make data-driven decisions.

### Problem 5: Service and Staff Management Complexity
The services catalog and employee management features streamline service offerings, pricing, and staff assignments, making operations more efficient and organized.

## Key Features
- Booking system
- Calendar management
- Services catalog
- Employee management
- Shift scheduling
- Customer database
- Billing and payments
- Business reports and analytics
