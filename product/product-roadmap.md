# Product Roadmap

## Sections

### 1. Bookings
Core booking system with calendar view and services catalog for managing appointments and service offerings.

### 2. Team
Employee management and shift scheduling to organize staff, availability, and coverage.

### 3. Customers
Customer database to track profiles, service history, and preferences for personalized experiences.

### 4. Business
Billing, payments, and analytics to track revenue, service performance, and business insights.
